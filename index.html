About Us

About our Cafe!

Ambience

You can call it ‘Paradise’ or ‘Swargam’ or ‘Jannat’. We call it a place to unwind. Come discover our unique QISSA, prepared with the ingredients of passion flavor and mood!.

The word Qissa

There’s a story everywhere and in everything,QISSA the land of stories narrated on a wide platter of delights! In ‘Persian’ means Story.

Serves

Continental Cuisine and freshly baked Cakes and Pies with Mock tails All Day.

Our Services

Mocktails

We serve in a healthy way, which provide you with all of the benefits of the ingredients we use.

Continental cuisine

Dishes that are made by stewing, grilling and roasting method are low in calories, as they make very less use of fats.

TakeAway!

You can order the same quality food from the cafe and it will be packaged in a manner that retains the heat and ensures that it comes to you hot and fresh.
